What is Woogle?
A search engine, which tries to index all wicket related stuff for a more dedicated result.
My first test of Nutch.
What technologies is used?
Nutch (Lucene), Wicket, Hibernate and Spring
What are indexed?
  • http://ginandtonic.org/wicket/ (IRC logs)
  • http://www.wicket-wiki.org.uk/wiki/index.php/Special:Allpages (Wiki)
  • http://wicket.sourceforge.net/apidocs/ (Javadocs)
  • http://www.mail-archive.com/wicket-user@lists.sourceforge.net/ (User mailing list)
  • http://www.mail-archive.com/wicket-develop@lists.sourceforge.net/ (Developer mailing list)
  • http://www.wicketframework.org/ (Website)
How often are they indexed?
Once every day.
